AASF is Abhigyan Abhikaushalam Students' Forum.

The AASF, or Abhigyan Abhikaushalam Students’ Forum, is a prominent student-run club at ABV-IIITM Gwalior that blends theoretical knowledge with practical skills to foster excellence and innovation among students.

What the club is: AASF cultivates a holistic environment where students develop technical acumen and managerial prowess through peer-to-peer learning, mentorship, and hands-on activities, preparing members for academic and industry challenges.

Key features and offerings: Knowledge-and-skills integration to make students industry-ready; strong peer-to-peer learning and mentorship from senior members; events and workshops such as Mock Interview Series (placement/interview practice with feedback), Experientia (resume and career-insight sessions), Winter Projects (mentored domain projects), Webkriti (web-development team projects), Aakriti (graphic-design competition), and git init FOSS (open-source contribution guidance).

Club content and activities: Activities span web development, machine learning, blockchain, graphic design, and open-source development; the club runs collaborative, project-based initiatives and provides platforms for regular workshops, speaker sessions, and competitions.

Legacy and community: AASF also publishes an annual magazine (Abhishar) honoring past members and sharing stories, achievements and inspiration, reinforcing the club’s culture of continuity, learning, and community impact.
